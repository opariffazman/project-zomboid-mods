description=[b]Grab your bucket & scoop em![/b]
description=
description=[h2]Problem[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Farming in zomboid get tedious once your water depleted and need a refill.
description=[*]Filling water from barrels, lake & river feels the same with all water containers.
description=[*]Even containers like bucket or bowl where in real life we can just scoop em!
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Solution[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]This mod make containers like bucket or bowl able to take water faster.
description=[*]Bottle is still faster than normal but slower than a bucket or a cup.
description=[*]As long as water is being taken from barrels, lake or river.
description=[*]Tags are added to water containers for different scooping speed.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Installation[/h2]
description=Subscribe to this mod normally and enable it.
description=

description=Build 41.78+
