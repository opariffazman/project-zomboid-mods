title=ItemTagsCompatibilityPatch
description=Compatibility patches for various other mods that uses hardcoded item name, this mod patched them to use in-game existing tags instead.
description=
description=For example, items added by [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2870742794]Tools of The Trade[/url] correctly uses in-game [b]Tags[/b]. Say a Spiffo Army Knife which got various function, it got "Scissors" and "SewingNeedle" tags.
description=However other mods like [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2754567348]Noir's Attachments[/url] only works if you got "Scissors" & "Needle" tools specifically. So tools from other mods that are tagged correctly won't be usable.
description=
description=This mod fixes that. If you use other mods that add items with proper use of in-game [b]Tags[/b] (Screwdriver, SharpKnife, etc).
description=Recipes or actions added by these patched mods will correctly recognize the items added. [i]Note: It is not limited to [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2870742794]Tools of The Trade[/url] items as this is just what I use.[/i]
