title=Item Tags Compatibility Patch
description=[b][h1]ITCP - Item Tags Compatibility Patch[/h1][/b]
description=[b]Compatibility patches for various other mods that uses hardcoded item name, this mod patches them to use in-game existing tags instead.[/b]
description=
description=[h2]Problem[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Items added by [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=2870742794]Tools of The Trade[/url] correctly uses in-game [b]Tags[/b].
description=[*]The Spiffo Army Knife has [i]"Scissors"[/i] and [i]"SewingNeedle"[/i] [b]Tags[/b].
description=[*]But recipes from mods [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=2754567348]Noir's Attachments[/url] won't work with it.
description=[*]As it will only works if you specifically have these items [i]"Scissors"[/i] & [i]"Needle"[/i].
description=[*]So tools from other mods that are tagged correctly won't be usable with other mods that have hardcoded values.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Solution[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]This mod fixes that by using the corresponding [b]Tags[/b] instead.
description=[*]Other mod items that uses in-game [b]Tags[/b] will also be usable with these patched mods.
description=[*]Recipes or actions added by these patched mods will correctly recognize the items added.
description=[*]Note: It is not limited to [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=2870742794]Tools of The Trade[/url] [i](This is just what I use.)[/i]
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Installation[/h2]
description=Subscribe to this mod normally and enable the patches that you need.
description=
